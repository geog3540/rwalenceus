University of Iowa Earth and Environmental Sciences

My name is Rachel, and I am an undergraduate student at the University of Iowa. I study Geoscience (B.S.) and Geographic Informational Systems (GIS). I am involved in research at the Ashton Prairie Living Laboratory, where I study the soil health and chemistry. Check out my poster from the Student Undergraduate Research Festival 2023--link at the bottom! I am also an intern at the Iowa Geological Survey, where I am a research assistant. Projects I have been involved in range from hydrogeology to quaternary geomorphological interactions. I am currently working on the relationship of loess-paleosol interactions of the Iowan Erosion Surface for the LoessFest 2024 summer conference held in Mainz, Germany. Stay tuned for poster updates! This project will also carry over to being my Senior Thesis; I look forward to the academic challenge! I am currently planning to attend graduate school and have been searching for universities with programs regarding coastal or fluvial geomorphology.
